# Comparison One — Native Static Image Ad Prompts 1080×1350

Campaign: Government-backed zero-interest SME loan awareness  
Format: 1080×1350 vertical feed, works on mobile and desktop  
Direction: Native “First 25” style frameworks, not polished generic fintech ads  
Use: Canva / Penpot / ChatGPT Images / Hermes image generation

## Production Note

These should feel more like native feed artifacts than finance-company ads.

Best build method:

1. Generate the background/layout without readable text if using AI image generation.
2. Add all text manually in Canva/Penpot/PIL.
3. Keep the Comparison One logo small or absent on the first version. Too much branding can make it look like an obvious ad.
4. Use a small CTA strip/button at the bottom, not a huge sales banner.

Canvas: `1080×1350`  
Safe margins: 80 px left/right, 90 px top, 110 px bottom  
Palette: deep green `#074C3E`, dark `#03211B`, gold `#FCB650`, cream `#F5F6F0`, black `#111111`

## Ad 1 — Claim

### Framework

Claim / contrarian static.

### Why it works

Big, simple contradiction. It interrupts anyone who thinks a zero-interest loan is automatically the best option.

### Manual overlay copy

```text
0% interest
is not the whole story
```

Support line:

```text
Eligibility, purpose and lender fit still matter.
```

CTA strip:

```text
Check your funding path in 90 seconds
```

### Layout

- Cream or off-white background.
- Huge black headline, left aligned.
- “0%” in gold or green highlight.
- One small abstract finance-path icon or percent symbol card on the right.
- Bottom deep-green CTA strip with gold arrow.
- Minimal logo, bottom-right or top-left.

### Image generation prompt

```text
Create a 1080×1350 vertical static ad background for an Australian SME finance comparison brand. Native social feed style, bold claim-ad layout, off-white cream background, deep forest green and muted gold accents, minimalist editorial design. Include a large blank headline area on the upper-left, a simple abstract gold percent symbol card and branching pathway icon on the right, and a deep green empty CTA strip along the bottom. Do not include any readable text, logos, bank branding, government crests, official seals, fake documents, watermarks, or small illegible labels. Clean, sharp, mobile-first, slightly raw/native rather than polished corporate.
```

### Canva/Penpot prompt

```text
Create a 1080×1350 static Meta ad using a native claim-ad framework. Off-white background, huge left-aligned black headline, one gold highlight on “0%”, small abstract finance-path illustration on the right, and a deep-green CTA strip at the bottom. Use this copy:

Headline: 0% interest is not the whole story
Support: Eligibility, purpose and lender fit still matter.
CTA: Check your funding path in 90 seconds

Make it look like a high-performing native feed ad, not a bank ad. Minimal branding, strong contrast, lots of negative space.
```

## What Not To Do

- Do not make these look too premium or corporate.
- Do not overuse photos of smiling business owners.
- Do not create fake government or bank screenshots.
- Do not use fake reviews or star ratings.
- Do not write “free money”.
- Do not imply everyone qualifies.
- Do not add visible compliance boilerplate.
- Do not cram comparison tables onto the image.
