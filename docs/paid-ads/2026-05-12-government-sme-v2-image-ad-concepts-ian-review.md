# Static Image Ad Concepts — Government SME Zero-Interest Loan Awareness V2

Campaign: Government-backed zero-interest SME loan awareness
Destination: `/advertorial/zero-interest-business-loans-australia` → `/quiz`
Primary CTA: `Check what funding may fit`
Use: Ian review, static image production spec

## Brand/Production Notes

- Palette: deep green `#074C3E`, gold `#FCB650`, off-white `#F5F3EF`, charcoal `#2D2D2D`, sage `#8CB8A8`.
- Style: premium Australian SME finance comparison. Useful, practical, not bank-stock, crypto, bro-finance, or fake government.
- Font: Inter or similar. Heavy headline, medium supporting copy. Mobile-first, 3-8 words on the main line.
- Logo: Comparison One lockup small in footer or top-left. No bank logos, lender logos, official crests, fake seals, fake newspaper mastheads, fake government pages.
- Public disclaimer: none by default. Soften claims inside copy instead of adding boilerplate.
- Production rule: generate backgrounds without embedded text, then add all text manually in Canva/Figma/Penpot/PIL.

## Concept 1 — Claim Card: 0% Is Not The Whole Story

- Angle: contrarian awareness hook.
- Format(s): 1:1 feed, 4:5 feed, 9:16 Story/Reels cover.
- Framework: Claim.
- Visual: deep green editorial card with a large gold percent icon on one side and a branching funding-path icon on the other. High contrast, minimal, no people.
- On-image headline: `0% interest is not the whole story`
- Supporting line: `Purpose, timing and lender fit still matter.`
- CTA treatment: gold pill, `Check funding fit`
- Layout:
  - Top-left: C1 logo, small.
  - Centre: oversized headline, 2 lines max.
  - Right/lower: gold percent icon partially transitioning into branching pathway.
  - Bottom: CTA pill.
- Pair with copy variant: Problem Aware or Shorter Version.
- Compliance note: safe because it does not imply approval, eligibility, savings, or government affiliation.
- Image generation prompt:
  ```text
  Text-free 4:5 vertical background for an Australian SME finance ad. Deep forest green fintech editorial card, muted gold percent icon on the left, abstract branching funding-path icon on the right, premium clean lighting, high contrast, lots of negative space for manual headline text, no readable words, no logos, no government crests, no bank branding, no seals, no fake documents, no people, calm and credible.
  ```

## Resize Instructions

- 1:1: centre headline and keep CTA above bottom safe area. Use 48-72 px headline size depending on length.
- 4:5: preferred feed format. Give more vertical breathing room and place CTA in bottom 20%.
- 9:16: use as Story/Reels cover. Keep headline in upper third, CTA above bottom UI safe zone, logo small.
