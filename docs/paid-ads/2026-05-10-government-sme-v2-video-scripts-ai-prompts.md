# Comparison One — 45–90s Video Scripts + AI Scene Prompts

Campaign: Government SME awareness-first funding campaign  
Format: voiceover + animated/slideshow visuals, no full AI spokesperson  
Allowed spokesperson use: optional 0:00–0:03 hook only  
Destination: `/advertorial/zero-interest-business-loans-australia` → `/quiz`  
Primary CTA: `Check what funding may fit`  


## Model Recommendation

For this style, do **not** rely on one AI video model for the whole ad. The best result is a simple production stack:

### Recommended stack

1. **Image generation:** Midjourney, Ideogram, or Recraft for clean fintech-style still frames.
2. **Motion/video generation:** Runway or Kling for short 3–6 second scene clips and subtle motion.
3. **Edit/assembly:** CapCut, Premiere, DaVinci, Canva, or After Effects for slideshow pacing, captions, transitions, and brand cards.
4. **Voiceover:** ElevenLabs or a clean Australian VO recording.
5. **Final captions:** Burned-in subtitles, not platform auto-captions only.

### Best practical choice

If you want one main AI video model for the scene motion, use **Runway** for polished brand-safe clips and controlled motion. Use **Kling** if you want more realistic business B-roll from text prompts. For this campaign, most scenes should be static/animated cards, so the edit system matters more than the video model.

### Prompt style to use

Prompt for **short clips**, not full ads:

- 3–5 second motion clip.
- No speech.
- No text inside generated footage unless you add it manually in editing.
- Leave negative space for overlay text.
- Avoid logos, fake lender names, and government crests.
- Use realistic Australian SME environments.

## Editing Template

For all five ads:

- 0:00–0:03: hard hook with optional AI spokesperson or kinetic text.
- 0:03–0:20: explain the zero-interest loan.
- 0:20–0:40: make the offer attractive, then introduce eligibility reality.
- 0:40–0:65: show why funding fit matters.
- 0:65–0:90: C1 matching system and CTA.

## Final CTA Variants

Use these rather than risky finance CTAs:

- `Check what funding may fit`
- `Compare funding options`
- `Start your funding-fit check`
- `See lender options that may suit`

Avoid:

- `Get approved`
- `Guaranteed funding`
- `Everyone qualifies`
- `See if you qualify` as a Meta CTA button
- `Free money`
