# Video Ad Scripts — Government SME Zero-Interest Loan Awareness V2

Campaign: Government-backed zero-interest SME loan awareness
Destination: `/advertorial/zero-interest-business-loans-australia` → `/quiz`
Primary CTA: `Check what funding may fit`
Use: Ian review, short-form video scripts/specs

## Production Assumptions

- Format: 9:16 Reels/Stories first, cut to 4:5 feed where useful.
- Production style: ElevenLabs or clean Australian VO + animated slideshow/cards + selected Kling/Runway motion hooks.
- Talent: no AI spokesperson needed. If used, limit to 0:00-0:03 hook only, then cut to text cards and SME finance visuals.
- Text: add all overlays manually. Do not ask AI image/video tools to render readable words.
- Tone: useful finance-news explainer, direct response, not hype, not fear-based.
- CTA: 90-second funding-fit check, no credit pull for the initial check.

## Edit Notes

- First 3 seconds: hard cut, headline appears within 0.3 seconds, no slow fade-in.
- Overlay text: huge, 3-7 words, cream or charcoal. Gold only for emphasis, CTA, chips, icons.
- Captions: bottom-safe, readable, not covering CTA.
- Sound: subtle finance-news hit on first frame, soft ticks for checklist/pathway animation, no corporate ukulele.
- Motion: use Kling/Runway for 3-5s hook clips only. Use still frames plus CapCut/Premiere/DaVinci motion for the rest.
- Export safe zones: keep CTA above platform UI area in Stories/Reels.
