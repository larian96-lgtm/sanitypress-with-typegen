## Production Assumptions

- **Format:** Vertical 9:16 video (1080×1920), 30fps
- **Destination:** Meta Ads Manager — Reels, Stories, Feed placement
- **CTA destination:** `/advertorial/zero-interest-loan-access-gap` → `/quiz`
- **Talent:** Voiceover (VO) only for 15s versions; optional on-camera narrator for 30s versions if founder/trusted-voice asset available
- **Asset needs:**
  - Stock footage or simple animated graphics (B-roll elements specified per script)
  - Comparison One logo end card
  - Caution/educational tone — no high-pressure music or flashing text
  - Font: Sans-serif, large enough for mobile mute playback
- **Music:** Low-urgency, steady tempo — warm acoustic or minimalist synth. No alarm, countdown, or "urgent" tones.
- **Colour grade:** Warm, natural, daylight feel — avoid greyscale/hardship visual language
- **Mute-safe:** All scripts work with captions only — VO supports but is not required

## Production Checklist

### Required Assets

- [ ] Comparison One brand kit (logo, colours, fonts)
- [ ] Product icons: Working Capital, Invoice Finance, Equipment Finance, Unsecured Loan (from C1 brand kit)
- [ ] Stock footage: SME owner (40s–60s, practical workwear), workshop/warehouse/desk setting, warm natural lighting
- [ ] Stock footage: hands with documents, calendar, phone, laptop
- [ ] Stock or animated elements: diverging paths, checklist, timeline, Australia map with sectors, product card flips
- [ ] Music track: low-urgency, warm acoustic or minimalist synth (no countdowns, no alarms, no high-tempo)
- [ ] Voiceover recording: neutral Australian accent, calm and authoritative tone (not salesman-like)
- [ ] End card template (brand card with CTA button + disclaimer space)
- [ ] Caption file (.SRT or baked-in) for each variant

### Filming Notes

- SME owner talent: dress in practical, sector-neutral workwear (no suit/tie, no hi-vis that suggests a specific industry). Think: checked shirt, jeans, or casual jacket.
- Lighting: warm daylight temperature (4500–5000K). Avoid harsh overhead fluorescents or dark shadows.
- Setting: workshop, warehouse, packaging bench, or simple office with natural materials (wood, concrete, plants). No grey cubicles or sterile boardrooms.
- Expression range: thoughtful, mildly curious, quietly confident. No smiling-at-camera sales vibes. No concerned/angst expressions.
- Phone/laptop screens: use screen mock-ups, not actual phone recordings (avoid reflections and lighting inconsistency).

### Edit Notes

- **Pacing:** 2–3 seconds per shot in 15s versions. 3–5 seconds in 30s versions.
- **Transitions:** Cross-dissolve or gentle push. No flash transitions, glitch effects, or zoom bumps.
- **Captions:** Baked-in burn-in captions required for mute-safe playback. Sans-serif font, white with 15% black stroke. Max 2 lines on screen at a time. Sync each line to spoken VO or shot change.
- **Colour grade:** Warm +0.05 in temperature, contrast +5, shadows up +3. No teal/orange blockbuster grade. No desaturation that reads as "grey/sad".
- **Music mix:** -18dB to -22dB under VO. Duck VO sidechain if music has rhythmic elements.
- **End card hold:** Minimum 2s in 15s versions, 3s in 30s versions. CTA button should be tappable — full visible area.
- **Disclaimer text:** Must be readable at mobile size (minimum 24px). Place bottom third, not overlaid on CTA button.
