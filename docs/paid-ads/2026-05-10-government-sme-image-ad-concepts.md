## Brand & Production Notes

- **Primary palette:** Deep green `#074C3E` (backgrounds, bold headers), gold `#FCB650` (CTAs, highlights, accents), off-white `#F5F3EF` (clean backgrounds), charcoal `#2D2D2D` (body text), soft sage `#8CB8A8` (secondary accents).
- **Font/style direction:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar). Heavy weight for headlines (700+), medium weight for body (500). No script, decorative, or slab serif. Rounded buttons for CTAs.
- **Logo usage:** Comparison One logo (lockup) in footer or top-left corner. Small size, no prominence over the message. Use white logo on dark backgrounds, charcoal logo on light backgrounds.
- **Imagery style:** Real Australian SME environments — workshop bench, cafe counter, ute/van, stockroom, invoice stack on desk, laptop with cash-flow dashboard. Warm natural light. No stock-photo "professional handshake" corporate imagery. No distress or hardship cues. Calm, practical, decision-support mood.
- **Illustration style (where used):** Clean geometric line-art icons in deep green/sage on off-white. Thin 2px strokes, rounded caps. No flat-filled illustration or isometric 3D. Consistent with C1 web illustration system.
- **Disclaimer:** "General information only. Comparison One is not a lender or a government agency." — always present as a 6pt line at bottom of every static ad. In carousels, appears on Card 1 and Card 5.
- **Required disclaimer text:** "General information only. Comparison One is not a lender or government agency." Must be legible at 6pt on mobile. Use `#6B7280` grey on a 12px-high band at bottom edge.

## Resize Instructions

### Aspect Ratio Reference

| Format | Dimensions | Canvas ratio | Use case |
|--------|-----------|-------------|----------|
| 1:1 | 1080 × 1080 | Square | Feed — primary format |
| 4:5 | 1080 × 1350 | Portrait | Feed — taller mobile format |
| 9:16 | 1080 × 1920 | Full portrait | Stories, Reels cover |

### General Resize Rules

1. **Start from 1:1 as master canvas.** Design the 1:1 layout first. Crop/extend for 4:5 and 9:16 — do not redesign.
2. **For Concepts 1, 3 (photography-based):**
   - 1:1 → 4:5: Add 135px of content/negative space above and below. For photo-based concepts, extend the gradient overlay rather than stretching the image. Use a wider crop of the original photo.
   - 1:1 → 9:16: Add 420px above and below. For photo-based: extend the image blur/colored background band above and below the main image. Keep headline in the lower third safe zone (bottom 600px).
   - **Safe zone for text:** Top 100px and bottom 150px should be clean background or gradient overlay — no critical image detail here.
3. **For Concepts 2, 4, 5, 6 (illustration/design-based):**
   - These are vector-constructed. Export at each ratio directly from design tool.
   - Maintain consistent padding ratios: 8% top margin, 8% bottom margin, 10% side margins.
   - 4:5: Reduce icon size by ~10% to accommodate taller canvas. Move CTA button down proportionally.
   - 9:16: Major vertical compression — reduce icon size by 25%, stack headline → supporting → CTA tighter. Consider reducing headline font size by 20% to fit.
4. **Carousels:** Build as native 1:1 cards. Carousels are 1:1 only in feed. If Stories/Reels carousel is needed, rebuild at 9:16 with the same card structure but vertical stack layout.
5. **Text sizing floor:** Never go below 20pt for on-image text on 1:1 (scales proportionally for 4:5 and 9:16). Body/supporting text minimum: 16pt on 1:1. Disclaimer: 6pt minimum at all sizes.
