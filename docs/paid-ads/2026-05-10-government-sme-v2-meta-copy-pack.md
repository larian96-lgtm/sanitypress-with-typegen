# Meta Ads Copy Pack V2 — Government SME Awareness-First Campaign

## Campaign Setup

- **Campaign:** Government SME Awareness-First V2 — C1 Meta Launch
- **Destination:** `https://comparison-one-sanitypress.vercel.app/advertorial/zero-interest-business-loans-australia`
- **Objective:** Traffic → Advertorial engagement → Quiz start / lender match (primary conversion)
- **Primary CTA:** `Check what funding may fit`
- **Alternate CTAs:** `Learn More`, `Compare options`, `Check my options`, `Check what funding may fit`
- **Recommended audience mode:** Interest-based + broad (Australia-wide, SME owner/operator signals, no credit/income attribute targeting)

## Rejected / Risky Copy

| Rejected copy | Reason | Risk level |
|---|---|---|
| "See if you qualify" | Implies C1 can determine eligibility for programs/lending | 🔴 High |
| "Check if your sector qualifies" | Personal eligibility framing | 🔴 High |
| "Get approved now" | Guarantee language | 🔴 High |
| "Free money from the government" | Misleading — loan must be repaid | 🔴 High |
| "The government will pay your interest" | Inaccurate framing | 🔴 High |
| "Guaranteed zero-interest funding" | Guarantee + implies all businesses qualify | 🔴 High |
| "We match you with guaranteed lenders" | Guarantee language | 🔴 High |
| "Bad credit OK for zero-interest loan" | Unsupported + credit attribute targeting | 🔴 High |
| "Can't access the program? We're your backup" | Exclusion framing + implies C1 is direct alternative | 🟠 Medium |

### Safe substitution examples

| Risky line | Safe alternative |
|---|---|
| "See if you qualify" | "Check what funding may fit your business" |
| "Get approved" | "Compare options that may suit your needs" |
| "Your sector won't qualify" | "The program targets specific sectors — check if yours is included" |
| "We'll find you a lender" | "Our system helps match your profile to lenders" |
