## Brand & Production Notes

- **Primary palette:** Deep green `#074C3E` (backgrounds, bold headers), gold `#FCB650` (CTAs, highlights, accents), off-white `#F5F3EF` (clean backgrounds), charcoal `#2D2D2D` (body text), soft sage `#8CB8A8` (secondary accents)
- **Font/style direction:** Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar). Heavy weight for headlines (700+), medium weight for body (500). No script, decorative, or slab serif
- **Logo usage:** Comparison One logo (lockup) in footer or top-left corner. Small size
- **Imagery style vs V1:** V2 shifts from "problem/gap" visuals to "awareness/education" visuals. More positive, news-oriented, explanatory. Real Australian SME environments. Calm, decision-support mood

## Static Image Ad Concepts (6)

### Concept 1 — "Did you know?" News Announcement Card

- **Angle:** Angle 1 — Awareness hook
- **Format(s):** 1:1 (1080×1080), 4:5 (1080×1350), 9:16 (1080×1920)
- **Visual:** A framed newspaper/news-clipping aesthetic on a warm background. A clean document mockup with big headline text like a news announcement. No actual news brand logos. Stylised "BREAKING" or "NEW" badge in gold on deep green background. Off-white paper texture background. Neutral, positive, informational.
- **On-image headline:** `Did you know?` (charcoal, weight 700, large)
- **Supporting line:** `Australian Government backs zero-interest SME loans.` (deep green, weight 600)
- **Sub-supporting:** `Not every business can access it. Here's what to know.` (sage green, weight 400)
- **CTA treatment:** Gold pill button `#FCB650`: `Learn More →`
- **Layout (1:1 stack):**
  - Top: News-badge + "Did you know?" headline (40% of canvas)
  - Middle: Supporting + sub-supporting text (30%)
  - Bottom: CTA + disclaimer (20%)
  - Logo: bottom-right
- **Image generation prompt:**
  ```
  Clean editorial-style card. Off-white #F5F3EF background. Top-left corner has a small gold #FCB650 badge reading "NEW" in charcoal. Large headline "Did you know?" in charcoal Inter weight 700, left-aligned. Below it, subhead in deep green #074C3E: "Australian Government backs zero-interest SME loans." Below that, a smaller line in sage #8CB8A8: "Not every business can access it. Here's what to know." Gold pill button at bottom. No news logos, no government crests. Clean, modern, editorial aesthetic. Warm lighting.
  ```
- **UTM content key:** `awareness_news_card_static_v1`
- **Filename base:** `gov_sme_v2_news_announcement`

### Concept 3 — Manufacturing Reality Check Card

- **Angle:** Angle 3 — Manufacturing doesn't auto-qualify
- **Format(s):** 1:1, 4:5, 9:16
- **Visual:** Warm photograph of a manufacturing workshop or factory floor (Australian industrial setting — not a sterile factory). Focus on machinery/equipment, not people. Soft natural light through industrial windows. The image should feel solid, real, and neutral — not distressed or negative. Overlaid with a semi-transparent deep green band at bottom for text.
- **On-image headline (over dark band):** `Manufacturing doesn't mean auto-qualify.` (white, weight 700)
- **Sub-supporting:** `Even target sectors go through lender assessment for zero-interest loans.` (white, weight 400)
- **CTA:** Gold pill `Compare options →`
- **Image generation prompt:**
  ```
  Warm photograph inside an Australian manufacturing workshop — industrial machinery, workbenches, metal fabrication equipment. Natural light through large factory windows. No people visible. Real working environment, not a sterile corporate factory. Slightly warm colour temperature. Shallow depth of field — foreground machinery in focus, background workshop blurred. 35mm film aesthetic. Moody but not dark or distressed. Green/cool colour palette overall. Suitable for text overlay on bottom 40%.
  ```
- **UTM content key:** `manufacturing_reality_check_static_v1`
- **Filename base:** `gov_sme_v2_manufacturing_reality`

### What to avoid
- ❌ No government crests, coat of arms, or "Australian Government" wordmark
- ❌ No lender logos
- ❌ No implication C1 is part of the program
- ❌ No implied approval or qualification certainty
- ❌ No distress imagery — positive/neutral decision-support mood only
- ❌ No "guaranteed", "bad credit accepted", "lowest rates"
- ❌ No all-caps headlines
- ❌ No emoji in on-image text
- ❌ No countdown timers or urgency elements
