# Comparison One — Video 01 Revised Script

Campaign: Government-backed zero-interest SME loan awareness  
Format: ElevenLabs VO + Kling/KIE.ai 3–5s hook + ChatGPT Images slideshow  
Length target: 75–90 seconds  
CTA: Click the link below / 90-second eligibility check  
Status: Ian-revised version, tightened for production

## What Ian Changed

- Made the hook sharper and more direct by asking about eligibility immediately.
- Named the relevant sectors earlier: supply chain, manufacturing, and other industries.
- Pulled the practical uses forward: cash flow, equipment, stock, supplier payments, growth plans.
- Added the key contrast with the Covid-era SME lending scheme.
- Made the bank bottleneck clearer: current big-bank partnership/access limits may exclude some SMEs even in relevant industries.
- Strengthened the strategic point: interest rate is not the only thing that matters; purpose, profile, timing, and eligibility criteria matter more.
- Changed the final CTA from general brand CTA to a more concrete action: click below, 90-second check, no credit pull.

## Stronger Hook Options

### Hook A — strongest direct-response version
Are you eligible for Australia’s new government-backed zero-interest business loans?

### Hook B — more curiosity/news angle
Australia has a new zero-interest business loan program, but many SMEs may not get through the banks.

### Hook C — more problem/solution angle
If your business needs funding, don’t just chase the lowest rate. Check the funding path that actually fits.

Recommended first test: **Hook A**. It is direct, simple, and creates an immediate reason to keep watching.
