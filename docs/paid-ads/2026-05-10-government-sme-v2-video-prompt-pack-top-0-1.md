# Comparison One — Video Prompt Pack

Campaign: Government-backed zero-interest SME loan awareness  
Goal: Scroll-stopping top 0.1% style creative while staying credible and finance-safe  
Format: 9:16 vertical Reels/Stories first, then cut down to 4:5 feed  
Production stack: ElevenLabs VO → Kling/KIE.ai 3–5s hook → ChatGPT Images slideshow frames → CapCut/Premiere/DaVinci assembly

## Creative Principle

The first 3 seconds must create a business-owner pattern interrupt:

- Not generic: “Need business funding?”
- Better: “0% interest is not the hard part.”
- Better: “Your industry may fit. Your bank pathway may not.”
- Best first test: “Are you eligible for Australia’s new zero-interest business loan?”

The ad should feel like finance news broke into the feed, then quickly become a practical funding-path explainer.

Do not generate readable text inside AI images or video clips. Add all text manually in the editor.

---

## Final Recommended Video Angle

### Working title

**0% Interest Isn’t The Whole Story**

### Why this should be the first test

This is more scroll-stopping than a generic eligibility explainer because it creates a contradiction:

> “0% sounds amazing, but that is not the part that decides whether you can actually use it.”

That tension gives people a reason to keep watching.

## ElevenLabs Voice Prompt

Use this direction in ElevenLabs:

```text
Australian business finance narrator. Calm, clear, credible, slightly urgent in the first sentence, then practical and helpful. Not hype, not influencer-style, not radio-ad salesy. Speak like a finance explainer giving SME owners useful information they may have missed. Pace: brisk but understandable, around 145 words per minute. Strong emphasis on: “zero-interest”, “not every business”, “major-bank criteria”, “90 seconds”, and “no credit pull”.
```

### Recommended VO Script — 60–70s cut

```text
Are you eligible for Australia’s new government-backed zero-interest business loan?

If you’re in supply chain, manufacturing, or a related industry, this is worth checking.

Funds may be available for cash flow, equipment, stock, supplier payments, or growth plans.

But here’s the part most people miss.

Zero-interest does not mean automatic.

Unlike the Covid-era SME lending scheme, access is currently tied to participating major banks.

That means your industry might fit, but your pathway through the bank may not.

And if that pathway is off the table, funding may still be possible through other products.

There’s more to a business loan than the interest rate.

Your purpose, profile, timing, and lender fit can matter more than the headline rate.

Comparison One helps Australian SME owners check what funding may fit — across government-backed programs, business loans, lines of credit, equipment finance, invoice finance, and other lender options.

Click below. It takes around 90 seconds to check your options, with no credit pull on your file.
```

## Overlay Timing And Editing Notes

### First 3 seconds

Make this brutal and fast:

- 0:00.0: Hard cut into Kling clip.
- 0:00.2: Overlay appears: `Are you eligible?`
- 0:01.2: Second line appears: `Zero-interest SME loans`
- 0:02.4: Micro-cut/zoom, not a fade.
- 0:03.0: Cut into slideshow frame 1.

### Text style

- Use huge headline text, 3–7 words.
- Cream text on deep green.
- Gold only for CTA, icons, underlines, and chips.
- Captions should be clean and low on frame, not covering CTA.

### Sound design

- Subtle finance-news hit at 0:00.
- Soft tick/click sounds when checklist cards appear.
- Low ambient bed, not corporate ukulele, not hype music.

### Edit rhythm

- First 8 seconds: cuts every 1.5–2.5 seconds.
- Middle section: cuts every 4–6 seconds.
- CTA: hold for at least 3 seconds so people can read.

## What To Avoid

- Do not say “free money”.
- Do not imply everyone qualifies.
- Do not use fake government pages or official-looking documents.
- Do not use fake bank logos.
- Do not make the SME owner look desperate or ashamed.
- Do not use tiny on-screen tables.
- Do not put compliance boilerplate on public frames.
- Do not make the full ad in Kling. Use Kling only for hook/motion moments.
