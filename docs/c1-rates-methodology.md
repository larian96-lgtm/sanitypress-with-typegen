# Comparison One rate methodology

## Scope

This page explains how Comparison One presents lender rate data on business-loan pages.

## Data sources

Rate fields are captured from publicly available lender pages, product disclosures, and provider comparison pages where current pricing ranges are published.

## What the rate means

- `Rate from` is the advertised entry rate where available.
- `Rate to` is shown when lenders publish a range.
- Invoice/debtor products may use percentage-of-invoice style pricing instead of standard p.a. pricing.

## Important caveat

Rates shown are indicative public rates only. Final pricing depends on lender assessment and can vary based on:

- security offered
- business turnover and trading history
- industry risk
- credit profile
- product structure and repayment frequency
- fees and commissions

## Freshness standard

- Target update cadence: every 14 days.
- Public badge rule:
  - `Rates updated [date]` when freshest table date is within 30 days.
  - `Rates being reviewed` when freshest table date is older than 30 days.

## Editorial and compliance

Comparison One is not a lender and does not provide financial, legal, tax, or credit advice. Information is general only and should be checked against current lender terms before application.
